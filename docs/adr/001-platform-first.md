## Title

Platform-First Architecture

## Status

Accepted

...

## Decision

The solution will be architected as a single Volunteer Platform composed of reusable business services.

Conversational AI will be one user experience within the platform rather than the platform itself.

Business capabilities will be implemented as reusable services that may be consumed by conversational interfaces, web applications, scheduled automations, and future user experiences.
