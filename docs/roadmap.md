# Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap describes the planned evolution of the Juliet's House Volunteer Platform.

Roadmap phases represent the progressive maturity of business capabilities rather than fixed delivery dates. Priorities may change as additional stakeholder feedback and organizational requirements are discovered.

---

## Audience

- Leadership
- Project Stakeholders
- Architects
- Developers

---

# Phase 1 — Foundation

Establish the core platform capabilities required to support mobile rescue operations and future platform expansion.

### Business Capabilities

- Mobile Field Observation
- Observation Review
- Observation Publishing
- Animal Record Updates
- Organizational Knowledge Search

### Platform Capabilities

- Human Approval Workflow
- Google Workspace Publishing
- Pawlytics Integration
- Architecture Documentation

---

# Phase 2 — Operational Workflows

Expand support for day-to-day rescue operations beyond field observations.

### Business Capabilities

- Official Animal Intake
- Foster Management
- Adoption Assistance
- Volunteer Coordination
- Organizational Notifications

### Platform Capabilities

- Mailchimp Integration
- Additional Workflow Automation
- Enhanced Knowledge Management

---

# Phase 3 — Operational Excellence

Provide administrative visibility, reporting, and platform management capabilities.

### Business Capabilities

- Reporting
- Analytics
- Administrative Workflows

### Platform Capabilities

- Administrative Portal
- Knowledge Base Management
- Platform Monitoring
- Operational Dashboards

---

# Phase 4 — Platform Expansion

Extend the platform to additional user experiences and organizational systems.

### User Experiences

- Volunteer Portal
- Mobile Applications
- Messaging Platform Integrations

### Integrations

- Additional Organizational Systems
- Future Third-Party Integrations

### Platform Evolution

- Additional AI Experiences
- Expanded Business Services
- Future Organizational Capabilities

---

# Roadmap Philosophy

The Volunteer Platform is intended to evolve incrementally through reusable business capabilities.

Each phase builds upon previously established architectural foundations while preserving:

- Human-centered workflows
- Reusable business services
- Separation of concerns
- Integration independence
- Long-term maintainability

Roadmap priorities will continue to evolve as additional stakeholder knowledge and organizational requirements are discovered.
