# 001 - Volunteer Task Coordination

## Status

Discovery

## Requested By

Susan

## Problem Statement

Volunteers need a lightweight method for coordinating work that occurs unpredictably without introducing another scheduling system.

## Current Understanding

Laundry coordination is the initial use case.

Business analysis suggests this may represent a broader capability for coordinating volunteer-owned tasks.

## Questions

- Is laundry the only use case?
- What other claimable work exists?
- What business artifact is required?
- What lifecycle should tasks follow?
- How should volunteers be notified?

## Architectural Impact

Unknown

Requires Discovery and Business Analysis.

## Next Steps

- Conduct stakeholder discovery.
- Define business capability.
- Determine architectural impacts.
